JustRun

JustRun is a SCI-FI game, where the player must survive and collect objects to finnish the round.

As a player you have the next abilities : WallRun, Side-Dash, Forward Dash, Double-Jump. The player must use these abilities to dodge enemies bullets, reach to the finish point and of course to find the objects they have to collect. The objects are hidden, so that means the player must search for them. JustRun is a dynamic game because of the abilities, and because they player can shoot while he's using these abilities.

At JustRun only one person works. That's Synnapse.
